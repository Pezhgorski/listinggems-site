AI Background Removal

Three AI models, one click

Choose the model that fits your workflow. Quality mode (BiRefNet) for complex edges like jewelry and glass. Fast mode (BiRefNet Lite) for high-volume batches. Balanced mode (BEN2) for everyday products.

  • Handles transparent, reflective, and furry edges

  • CPU fallback for machines without a dedicated GPU
  • Models download once, then everything runs offline
