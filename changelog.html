Changelog

Release notes and version history for ListingGems.

Subscribe via RSS →

v0.3.3

June 11, 2026
  • Instagram presets. Export your photos sized perfectly for Instagram, right alongside the other marketplaces. Instagram Post (4:5 portrait) fills the feed, and Instagram Reel (9:16 vertical) is ready for Reels and Stories.
  • Auto Apply turns on after your first result. Once you process a photo, Auto Apply switches on automatically so your settings carry straight through to the next shot — no need to flip it on yourself.

v0.3.2

June 6, 2026
  • Better shadows. Two new shadow styles give your products a more natural, grounded look. Drop Shadow casts a soft directional shadow behind the item — great for flat-lay shots — and is now the default. Floor Shadow grounds the product as if it's standing on a surface.
  • New sliders to fine-tune the effect: Distance controls how far the shadow falls, Softness sets how sharp or blurred the edge is, and Light Height raises or lowers the light source for the floor shadow.

v0.3.1

June 4, 2026
  • Activate a license without leaving the app. On a free trial and ready to buy? The trial banner's “Buy a license” link and the License button now open an activation screen where you can paste your key and unlock ListingGems on the spot — no reinstall needed after purchase. Don't have a key yet? The same screen links straight to checkout.

v0.3.0

June 3, 2026
  • Free trial. Not ready to buy? You can now try ListingGems free for 7 days — process your own photos and see the results before you pay a cent. Runs entirely on your machine, just like the paid version.
  • Your license now becomes permanently valid offline once you're past the refund window — after that point ListingGems no longer needs to check in online to keep working, so it stays usable even with no internet for good.
  • Hardened how licenses are validated under the hood for better security and reliability. Existing licenses migrate automatically the first time you open this version — no action needed.
  • Anonymous telemetry to help us improve. On first launch ListingGems shows a notice explaining that it sends opt-out anonymous usage telemetry — an install ID and event counts only, never your photos or license keys. It's on by default; you can turn it off any time from Settings.

v0.2.3

May 27, 2026
  • Fixes a memory leak that caused RAM usage to grow by hundreds of megabytes per processed photo on CPU mode. Affected Windows users without a supported GPU most. After this update, RAM usage stays stable across a long batch.

v0.2.2

May 27, 2026
  • ListingGems now accepts .jfif input files. Some phone cameras and web tools save JPEGs with the .jfif extension instead of .jpg — these now load directly without renaming.

v0.2.1

May 23, 2026
  • Color Select preview now matches what Apply will do. The overlay shows exactly which pixels will be affected, including ones outside the current foreground. Restore can highlight matching colors anywhere in the photo.
  • When Restore is active, the canvas swaps to show the full original image with a dim tint over removed areas, so you can see what is about to come back into the mask. Tolerance scrubbing stays instant.
  • Erase keeps the regular product display to focus on what is being subtracted — no distraction from background pixels.
  • Flood fill no longer treats the existing mask edge as a wall, so a color region can be reached even when it spans across the foreground.

v0.2.0

May 21, 2026
  • Mask brush now supports zoom and pan for pixel-precise edge work: Ctrl+scroll to zoom, middle-mouse drag to pan, and keyboard shortcuts (0 to reset, + / - to zoom). A live cursor coordinate readout helps you align edits.
  • Fixed brush erase and restore coordinates so paint lands exactly under the cursor when working on a cropped preview.
  • Brush mode menu renamed for clarity: Brush (previously Smart) and Color Select, with Brush as the new default.
  • Added a dismissable hint banner explaining the brush in both Studio and Quick modes.
  • Background removal model picker now hides models your GPU can't run (FP16 support detection), so you only see options that will work on your hardware.
  • HEIC files that are actually JPEGs (such as Samsung motion photos) now load correctly instead of failing.

v0.1.2

May 15, 2026
  • Background removal models now preload in the background after app startup, so the first photo you process no longer waits on model initialization.

v0.1.0 — Initial release

April 2026
  • Background removal powered by BiRefNet and BEN2 ONNX models, running locally on GPU or CPU — no cloud, no per-image fees.
  • Studio Mode for fine-grained editing: drop shadows, color and exposure correction, smart 4x upscaling, and a manual mask touch-up brush for refining edges.
  • Quick Mode for drag-and-drop batch processing with progress and remaining-time estimates.
  • Nine marketplace presets covering eBay, Etsy, Amazon, Shopify, Poshmark, Mercari, Depop, Facebook Marketplace, and a custom aspect ratio.
  • One-time purchase of $49 with no subscription, three device activations per license, and a 45-day money-back guarantee.
  • Runs fully offline after install — photos never leave your machine.
